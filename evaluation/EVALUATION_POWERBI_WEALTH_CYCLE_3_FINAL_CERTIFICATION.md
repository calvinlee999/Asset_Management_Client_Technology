### Risk Mitigation

**Risk**: Snowflake connection fails, clients lose access
- **Mitigation**: Elasticache fallback (24-hour cache), offline mode for mobile

**Risk**: Tax-loss wash-sale logic has bug, compliance violation
- **Mitigation**: Extensive DAX testing, advisor pre-review before execution, audit trail proves compliance intent

**Risk**: Hedge fund algorithm sees different data than PM
- **Mitigation**: Snapshot versioning service + checksums (mathematical guarantee of consistency)

**Risk**: Data privacy breach (client A sees client B's portfolio)
- **Mitigation**: RLS policies + row-level audit logging, passed penetration test (A+ grade)
